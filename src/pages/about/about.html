Information collection and use

This service is not collecting any personally identifiable information. The application allows the user to detect spam phone numbers. Incoming phone numbers from your smartphone are filtered, hashed (with the algorithm SHA-512), then compared to already reported spam from our database. For the filtering of the call logs the application requires the permission to read the call logs (android.permission.READ_CALL_LOG).
